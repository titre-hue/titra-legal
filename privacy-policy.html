What titra Does

titra is a GLP-1 medication tracking app that helps you log doses, track side effects, and monitor your treatment journey. Your health is personal, and we treat your data that way.

Data We Collect

titra stores your medication doses, side effects, weight entries, and app preferences. All data is stored locally on your device by default. If you enable Cloud Sync, your data is encrypted and stored via Supabase (hosted on AWS). We do not collect your name, email address, or any personal identifiers unless you create a Cloud Sync account.

Data We Do Not Collect

We do not access your camera, contacts, location, photos, or files. We do not sell your personal data, share it with advertisers, or transfer it to data brokers.

Health Data

titra processes sensitive health information including medication dosages, side effects, and body weight. This data is used solely to provide you with tracking and insight features within the app. Your personal records are never shared with third parties, advertisers, or data brokers.

Community Insights (Anonymous Data Sharing)

If you opt in to Community Insights, titra submits a weekly anonymous snapshot to help build real-world benchmarks for GLP-1 medications. This is entirely optional and can be toggled on or off at any time in Settings.

What is shared (all anonymous): Medication type and dose, weight change since starting (not your actual weight), BMI category (not exact BMI), side effect types and severity, gender and age range, adherence percentage, weekly goal, and if Health Sync is enabled: rounded averages of steps, sleep, heart rate, and active calories.

What is never shared: Your name, email, device ID, IP address, exact weight, height, or date of birth, free-text notes, location, or Supabase user ID. Community data uses a random anonymous cohort ID generated per app install â it is not linked to your user account in any way.

Community data is stored in a separate Supabase table with no connection to user accounts. Benchmark results are only shown when at least 10 anonymous users exist for a given medication and dose combination.

Anonymised, aggregated statistical insights derived from community data (for example, population-level trends in GLP-1 medication adherence or side effect timing) may be used for research purposes or shared with research partners. These insights are mathematically aggregated and cannot be used to identify any individual. No personal data, individual records, or identifiable health history is ever included.

Analytics

titra uses PostHog (EU Cloud) for product analytics to help us understand how features are used and improve the app. Analytics data includes feature usage events (e.g. "dose logged", "export used") and is associated with your Supabase user ID if you are signed in. No health data, medication details, or personal identifiers are included in analytics events. PostHog data is hosted in the EU for GDPR compliance.

Health Connect / Apple Health

If you connect Google Health Connect or Apple HealthKit, titra reads steps, active calories, resting heart rate, and sleep data from your device. This data is displayed locally in the app and, if you opt in to Community Insights, anonymised averages (rounded to broad buckets) may be included in your weekly community snapshot. Raw health data is never transmitted to our servers.
