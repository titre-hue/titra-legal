Privacy Policy

VintSnap — AI Listing Tool for Vinted Sellers
Last updated: 9 April 2026

VintSnap (“the App”) is operated by Brian Merlehan (“we”, “us”, “our”). This policy explains what data the App collects, how it is used, and your rights.

1. Data We Collect

Account information. When you sign up with Apple Sign-In (iOS), Google (Android), or email and password, we store your email address, display name, and a unique user ID via Firebase Authentication. This is required to save your credits and listing history.

Listing data. Titles, descriptions, categories, prices, and photo references for items you analyse are stored locally on your device. If you mark an item as sold, anonymised pricing data (brand, category, condition, sale price, region) is uploaded to our database to improve pricing suggestions for all users. This data is not linked to your identity.

Usage data. We store your credit balance, region preference, and currency preference. This data is synced to Firebase Firestore and cached locally.

Photos. Photos you take are processed on your device and sent to the Google Gemini API for AI analysis. Photos are not stored on our servers. Google may process photos in accordance with their own privacy policy.

Purchase data. If you buy credit packs, the Apple App Store (iOS) or Google Play (Android) handles the transaction. On iOS, purchases are processed through RevenueCat, which receives a purchase token and product ID to grant credits. On Android, Google Play Billing is used directly. We do not have access to your payment details on either platform.

Analytics and crash data. We use PostHog (EU-hosted) for product analytics and Sentry (EU-hosted) for crash reporting. These services collect anonymised usage patterns, device information, and crash logs to help us improve the App. On Android, AppsFlyer is used for attribution analytics. No personally identifiable information is shared with these services beyond what is necessary for their operation.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To provide AI-powered listing generation (via Google Gemini)
  • To look up market pricing (via eBay Browse API — no user data is sent, only search queries)
  • To manage your credit balance and purchase history
  • To improve pricing accuracy through anonymised crowdsourced sold-price data
  • To monitor app stability and fix crashes (via Sentry)
  • To understand how features are used and improve the App (via PostHog)

3. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services, each with their own privacy policies:

  • Firebase (Google) — Authentication, database. Privacy policy
  • Google Gemini API — AI photo analysis. Terms
  • eBay Browse API — Market pricing lookups. No user data is shared with eBay; only product search queries are sent using app-level credentials.
  • RevenueCat — In-app purchase management (iOS). Privacy policy
  • Google Play Billing — In-app purchases (Android). Privacy policy
  • PostHog — Product analytics (EU-hosted). Privacy policy
  • Sentry — Crash reporting (EU-hosted). Privacy policy
  • AppsFlyer — Attribution analytics (Android only). Privacy policy
  • Google ML Kit — On-device background removal (Android). Processing happens entirely on your device.

4. Data Storage and Security

Account and credit data is stored in Google Firebase (hosted in the EU/US). All data is transmitted over HTTPS. Listing history is stored locally on your device in an app-private directory. We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data.

5. Data Retention

Your account data is retained for as long as your account exists. Listing history is stored on your device and is removed when you uninstall the App or delete your account. Anonymised crowdsourced pricing data is retained indefinitely to improve the service.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Delete your account directly from the App (Settings > Delete Account)
  • Withdraw consent for data processing

You can delete your account at any time from the Settings screen within the App. This will permanently remove your profile, credit balance, and listing history from our servers. To exercise any other rights, contact us at Vintsnap-app@outlook.com.

7. Children’s Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will delete it.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The “Last updated” date at the top will reflect the most recent revision. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
